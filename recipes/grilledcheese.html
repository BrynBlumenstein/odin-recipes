Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Here's a classic recipe for a simple and delicious grilled cheese sandwich

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread (white, whole wheat, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 to 1 1/2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, Swiss, or your favorite melting cheese)
  • Optional additions: sliced tomatoes, cooked bacon, or ham

Steps

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  2. Spread a thin layer of softened butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place two slices of bread, butter-side down, on the hot surface.
  4. Quickly add your choice of cheese evenly on the slices of bread. If you like, add any optional additions like sliced tomatoes, cooked bacon, or ham.
  5. Top with the remaining slices of bread, butter-side facing up.
  6. Cook until the bottom of the sandwich is golden brown and the cheese starts to melt, usually about 3-4 minutes.
  7. Carefully flip the sandwich with a spatula and cook the other side until it's golden brown and the cheese is completely melted.
  8. Once both sides are golden and the cheese is melted, remove the sandwich from the heat.
  9. Let it cool for a minute before slicing it in half diagonally.
