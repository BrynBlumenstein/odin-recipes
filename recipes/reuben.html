Reuben Sandwich

Description

Here's a classic recipe for a delicious Reuben sandwich

Ingredients

  • 8 slices rye bread
  • 1 pound thinly sliced corned beef
  • 1 cup sauerkraut, drained
  • 8 slices Swiss cheese
  • 1/2 cup thousand island dressing
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened

Steps

  1. Preheat a griddle or a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Lay out 4 slices of rye bread and spread a generous amount of thousand island dressing on each slice.
  3. Layer corned beef, sauerkraut, and Swiss cheese evenly on each of the 4 slices.
  4. Top each sandwich with the remaining 4 slices of rye bread.
  5. Spread softened butter on the outer sides of each sandwich.
  6. Place the sandwiches on the preheated griddle or skillet and cook until the bread is toasted and the cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  7. Press the sandwiches down with a spatula as they cook to flatten them slightly.
  8. Once the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, remove the sandwiches from the griddle.
  9. Allow them to cool for a minute before slicing diagonally and serving.
